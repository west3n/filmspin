Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data FilmSpin processes, why it is needed, and what control you have over that data.

Last updated: February 17, 2026

1. Scope

FilmSpin is a no-account service for random movie discovery. This policy applies to the website and its API endpoints.

2. Data We Process

We do not collect account profiles or payment data. We may process technical data such as request timestamps, path, HTTP status, user language, and anonymized operational logs.

3. Why We Process It

Technical data is used for service stability, debugging, abuse prevention, and performance monitoring.

4. Local Storage

FilmSpin uses browser local storage for interface preferences (for example selected language and filter state). This data stays on your device unless you clear it.

5. Third-Party Data Providers

Movie metadata and ratings are retrieved from third-party providers. Their services process requests under their own privacy policies. FilmSpin does not sell your personal data.

6. Retention

Operational logs are kept only for as long as needed to maintain reliability and security. Cached movie data is technical and not tied to personal user accounts.

7. Your Choices

You can stop using the service at any time, clear your browser storage, and block requests to third-party services through browser tools or network settings.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect product, legal, or infrastructure changes. The date at the top shows the latest revision.
